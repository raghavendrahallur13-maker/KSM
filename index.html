Our Heritage

A Century of Cultural Contribution

For nearly nine decades, Karnataka Sahitya Mandira (KSM) has stood as a beacon of Kannada language and literature in the twin cities of Hyderabad and Secunderabad. Founded in 1936 by visionary cultural leaders, KSM has organically evolved into a vibrant community hub serving Kannadigas, Telugus, and Hindi speakers alike.

As a formally registered institution (Regn. No. 52 / 1952), our mission is simple yet profound: to honor our roots, elevate regional literary arts, and provide a dignified stage for cultural exchange. Today, our doors remain open to scholars, artists, and families who wish to connect with their heritage in a warm, welcoming environment.

The KSM Venue

Located in the heart of Hyderabad, the Karnataka Sahitya Mandira is more than just a building—it is a physical anchor for cultural pride. Our spacious premises regularly host large-scale cultural gatherings, intimate symposiums, health classes, and community meetings.

With an inviting atmosphere and decades of historical significance, the KSM venue serves as an ideal gathering space for the vibrant Kannada-speaking population and residents of the twin cities.
